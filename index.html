ADVOCACY
Fight For What's Right.

Disabled kids deserve equal treatment, equal opportunities, and equal respect. SU Kids fights for all of it.

  • ✨Equal access to education for every disabled kid
  • ✨Anti-bullying laws that protect disabled students
  • ✨More representation of disabled kids in media
  • ✨Affordable therapies and medical support for all families
  • ✨Workplaces that welcome disabled people as adults
